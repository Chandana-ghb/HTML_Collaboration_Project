About the Movie

A fiery Income Tax officer, Amay Patnaik, confronts the corrupt nexus in Bhoj. Beneath the veneer of honesty lies a sinister operation tied to Dada Bhai, a revered politician. As Amay unravels layers of deceit buried in fields and fortresses, one question looms: will justice prevail, or will power silence the truth?
