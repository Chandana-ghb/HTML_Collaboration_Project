About the Movie

Rahul, a 27-year-old MBA from Kanpur, finds himself torn between societal pressure and personal desire when he falls for Priya, a strong-willed MNC professional from Lucknow. While his spiritualist mother pushes for an arranged marriage, Rahul`s friend Raj urges him to enjoy life without commitment. As love blossoms between Rahul and Priya, conflicting beliefs about relationships spark tension. When a misunderstanding threatens to pull them apart, Rahul must confront his feelings, grow up, and choose between fleeting freedom and lasting love.
